How We Work

  1. Kickoff (30–45 mins): goals, pages, brand notes, examples.
  2. Name & hosting: register/transfer domain, DNS, SSL, CDN, backups.
  3. Design direction: moodboard + wireframes (homepage first).
  4. Build & content: pages, forms, CTAs; copy polish; imagery.
  5. Review & edits: 1–2 rounds; accessibility & performance pass.
  6. Go-live: DNS switch, Google index request, Business Profile, launch post.
  7. Handover: admin access + short video walkthrough.
